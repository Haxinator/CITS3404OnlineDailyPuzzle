About PicturePerfect

Theme

Colors
The puzzle colour pallet was specifically chosen to contrast for those who are colour blind. According to this site.
Style
Blend of retro game with modern minimalism.

Purpose

Learn pixel art?
See if you can recreate the given pixel art.

How to Play

The Overall Objective: copy the pattern.
In the puzzle page the first thing you will see is a canvas with a question mark on it. Press the start button to reveal the pattern. Take some time to memorise the pattern and when you feel ready press the ready button. The pattern will disappear and you must recreate it on the now blank canvas. You are given a pallete of colours, which appear below the puzzle, click on a colour to select it, then click on the canvas to apply the colour to the cell. You can also hold down click to colour multiple cells. If you want to remove a colour click on the eraser icon, found next to the colour pallet. Once confident in your answer press the check button. If you get the pattern right you will recieve a win screen. If you get it wrong you will recieve a game over screen, with the wrong cells highlighted.
