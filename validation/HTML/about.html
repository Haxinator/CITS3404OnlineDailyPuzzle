How to Play

Let's Begin: where are we dropping?

In the puzzle page the first thing you will see is a question mark. This indicates you haven't started the puzzle yet. Press the start button to reveal the pattern.

Overall Objective: get the number one Victory Royale.

Assuming you pressed start, you should now see the pattern. Take some time to memorise the pattern. When you feel ready press the ready button.

Gameplay: Chug Jug

After you press ready the pattern will disappear and you must recreate it in the canvas (area enclosed in a white border).



You are given a pallete of colours, which appear below the puzzle, click on a colour to select it, then click on the canvas to apply the colour to a cell. You can also hold down click to colour multiple cells.



If you want to remove a colour click on the eraser icon, in the color pallet and then click on the cell you want to remove. You can also hold down click to remove multiple colors. Alternatively if you have the same colour selected you can click on the cell again to remove the colour.



Once confident in your answer press the check button.

Oh No: my friend got downed

If you get the pattern wrong you will recieve a game over screen, with the wrong cells highlighted. You can then press start again to retry the puzzle. The puzzle will remember the cells you got correct, so the next time you press ready the correct cells will already have the right colours.

YOU WIN!: We got a number one Victory Royale...

If you get the pattern right you will recieve a win screen and the "Next" button will appear if there is another puzzle in the daily set to complete. If there isn't another puzzle in the set all that is shown is a win screen and text stating "Daily Puzzle Supply Depleted for now...".
